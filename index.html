ATR Tribal Reserve Guidelines

⚠️ MANDATORY COMPLIANCE: The Andaman Trunk Road (ATR) passes through the protected Jarawa Tribal Reserve. Strict laws apply.

1. Security Convoy Rules

  • Buses travel in coordinated convoys escorted by Police vehicles.
  • You must arrive at least 45 minutes before the scheduled convoy departure time at the checkpost.
  • Convoys depart from Jirkatang (South Andaman) and Middle Strait (Baratang).

2. Tribal reserve regulations (Very Important)

  • NO PHOTOGRAPHY OR VIDEOGRAPHY: Taking pictures or recording videos of tribal community members is a strict, non-bailable offense under the PAT regulation. Penalties include imprisonment and confiscation of equipment.
  • NO CONTACT: Do not attempt to interact, talk to, offer food, or throw objects/clothing at tribals.
  • WINDOWS CLOSED: Keep bus windows shut or pull up shutters when passing through the reserve forests.
  • NO OVERTAKING: Vehicles must remain in line and not halt or overtake inside the forest limit.
